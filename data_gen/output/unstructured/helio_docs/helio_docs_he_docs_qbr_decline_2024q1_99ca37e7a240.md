# Quarterly Business Review Notes for Helio Retail

## Executive Summary

The recent Quarterly Business Review for Helio Retail highlights a concerning trend of sustained decline in usage of our graph platform, leading to a plan downgrade and potential migration away from our services. This contraction poses a significant risk to the upcoming renewal, necessitating immediate attention and strategic intervention.

## Details

### Section 1

During the latest review, it was noted that Helio Retail's Net Promoter Score (NPS) and verbatim sentiment have both reached their lowest levels. This decline in customer satisfaction is alarming and indicates a growing disconnect between the platform's value and the customer's expectations. The feedback received suggests that the current offerings may not be meeting Helio Retail's evolving needs.

### Section 2

Usage metrics reveal a consistent quarter-over-quarter decline from the previous peak, underscoring a troubling trend in engagement with the graph platform. This sustained decrease in usage is a critical indicator that the account is not deriving the anticipated value from our services, which could lead to further disengagement if not addressed promptly.

### Section 3

In response to these challenges, Helio Retail has downgraded its plan to a lower tier, resulting in a reduced contract value. This decision reflects their current strategy to deprioritize the graph platform, and discussions around a potential migration away from our services have begun. Such actions not only threaten the immediate renewal but also jeopardize the long-term relationship we have built with this key account.

## Action Items

- Conduct a detailed analysis of usage metrics
- Engage with Helio Retail to understand their current needs
- Develop a tailored plan to address customer concerns
- Explore opportunities to enhance platform value for Helio Retail
